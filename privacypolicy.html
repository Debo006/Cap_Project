Privacy Policy

Protecting your data is our priority at Hisab Kitaab

Your data is never sold or shared with third parties. We only share anonymized insights for analytical improvement of our tools.

4. Security Measures

All your data is stored securely with encryption, and access is strictly limited to ensure safety and confidentiality.

5. Your Control

You can view, edit, or delete your data anytime through your dashboard or by contacting us directly.

We may update our privacy policy for better service. We will notify users in-app or via email when significant changes occur.
