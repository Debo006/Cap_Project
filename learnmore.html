What is Hisab Kitaab?

Hisab Kitaab is a simple tool designed to help shopkeepers, small business owners, and freelancers track their income and expenses with ease — no accounting knowledge needed.

What Can You Do With It?

  • ✅ Track sales and purchases daily
  • ✅ See monthly or custom period profit and loss
  • ✅ Understand your top expenses and income sources
  • ✅ Export and download reports for offline use
  • ✅ Use it on mobile, tablet, or desktop

Why Choose Hisab Kitaab?

- Clean and simple design — no confusion.
- Works offline too — ideal for rural or low-network areas.
- Safe and secure — your data is encrypted and private.

How to Get Started?

1. Click on Sign Up at the top right corner
2. Enter your basic details (name, phone/email)
3. Start entering your income and expense records
